Guinea Pig Care

Caring for guinea pigs requires attention to their dietary needs, living environment, and overall well-being. Here are some essential tips for providing proper care:

  • Provide a balanced diet with fresh hay, vegetables, and a small amount of guinea pig pellets.
  • Ensure a clean and spacious cage with appropriate bedding material.
  • Offer fresh water daily and monitor their health regularly.
