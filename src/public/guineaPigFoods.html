Guinea Pig Foods Guide

Guinea pigs enjoy a variety of foods, including fresh hay, vegetables, and high-quality guinea pig pellets. However, avoid giving them foods that are high in calcium.

Safe vegetables for guinea pigs include leafy greens, bell peppers, and carrots. Always introduce new foods gradually to prevent digestive issues.

Provide fresh water at all times and monitor your guinea pig's weight and overall health. Consult with a veterinarian for specific dietary recommendations.
