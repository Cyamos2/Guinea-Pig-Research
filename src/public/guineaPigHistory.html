Guinea Pig History

The history of guinea pigs dates back to ancient South America, where they were domesticated by indigenous people for food and cultural practices.

Spanish explorers introduced guinea pigs to Europe, where they became popular as exotic pets among the aristocracy in the 16th century.

Today, guinea pigs are cherished companions known for their gentle nature and social behavior. They have a rich history and have become beloved pets worldwide.
