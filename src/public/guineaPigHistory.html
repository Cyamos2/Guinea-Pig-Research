Guinea Pig History

Guinea pigs have a fascinating history that dates back to their introduction to Europe by Spanish conquistadors. Initially, they were kept as exotic pets by European royalty and later gained popularity among the general public.

During the Renaissance, guinea pigs were prized for their unique appearance and gentle temperament. They became favorite pets of European monarchs, including Queen Elizabeth I of England.
